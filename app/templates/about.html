About This Project

Silkworm Disease Prediction System

This web application uses machine learning to predict the occurrence of silkworm diseases based on climate parameters and management practices.

Research Background

The study was conducted in Ranchi, Jharkhand, India (23.3441°N, 85.3096°E) during October 2025. The research collected data from 8 different plots with varying spacing configurations and instar stages.

Diseases Monitored
  • Pebrine - A microsporidian disease caused by Nosema bombycis
  • Virosis - Viral infections affecting silkworms
  • Bacteriosis - Bacterial infections
  • Muscardine - Fungal disease caused by Beauveria bassiana
